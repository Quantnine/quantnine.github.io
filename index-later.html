Quantnine Research
Quantnine Research is a quantitative-driven crypto asset trading and technology firm that develops and deploys market-neutral algorithms based on statistical and mathematical models for trading in the global cryptocurrency market.

Quantnine Research is firmly believe that the future of finance will be decentralised and decentralised technology has the potential to revolutionise the finance industry by providing transparency, security and efficiency.

Quantnine Research is working to be a part of this evolution with the sophisticated algorithms it deploys..
